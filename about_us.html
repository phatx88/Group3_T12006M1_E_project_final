Story About Us

When we first joined the FPT Aptech computer education program at the beginning of summer 2020, we was not sure whether we could accommodate here. But our study journey here really gave us a good enjoyment of life. We became more confident that this was a right choice to join the Apteach institute. The facility was well-equiped and the staff was friendly and quite welcoming to newcomers.
Our first lesson here at the center was about how to think on our feet, it was not about knowing how to read or write a computer code but it was all about solving problems. We had a lots of discussion in class, where we would communicate our thoughts so freely together like never before. From here on, we started learn relevant knowledge about building/developing a computer program. Our instructor Mrs. Pham was very knowledgeable and passionate about teaching. The most impressive thing for us was that she would go great length to make sure each of us be able to keep up with our study.
We will behave in a manner so as to become a light, not a roadblock, for others who choose to follow or lead us. We will trust our dreams and be the prisoner of nothing. We will use our private victories unselfishly by trying to create value for others. The pursuit of excellence will determine the options we decide to exercise and the paths we choose to travel. We will expect no more of others than we expect of ourself.
We will seek new sources for learning and growth – nature, family, literature, new acquaintances. We will show love rather than expect love. We choose to focus upon being effective versus efficient. And finally, we choose to make a difference in this world.
